Reasons Why College is Important

With more and more occupations requiring advanced education, a college degree is critical to your success in today's workforce. But earning an accredited college degree can also have a significant impact on other areas of your life.

1. Earnings

On average, workers with a college degree earn almost $500.00 more than workers with just a college degree(BLS). This is resulted from the specialty focus you can gain from having a major rather than a broad high school diploma.

2. Unemployment

Workers with a college degree statistically have a higher employment rate than those without, because of companies looking for this with college degrees.

3. Job Satisfaction

Workers that have a college degree are less likely to be unhappy at a job, because if they are working in the field of what they majored in, they most likely have passion towards that subject.

4. Owning homes

Those with college degrees are more likely to own a house than those without. Zillow found that 75% of their homeowners have college degrees.

5. Marriage

78% of women with college degrees were found to be in healthy marriages, compared to 38% of high school graduate married women having healthy marriages.

6. Health

It has been proved that those with a college degree eat more vegetables, wear their seatbelt, and are overall more healthy.

7. Volunteering

40% of those with college degrees volunteered in their communities compared to only 17% of those with only high school diplomas volunteering
